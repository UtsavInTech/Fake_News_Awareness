How we help communities

We focus on local trust-building and practical skills so villagers, students, and community leaders can stop false stories from becoming crises.

Workshops in Schools

Hands-on sessions for students and teachers: spotting manipulated media, checking sources, and critical thinking exercises.

Verification Booths

Booths at markets and public gatherings where people can bring suspicious messages to get quick verification and guidance.

Volunteer Verification Teams

Local volunteers trained to triage rumours, contact officials, and publish verified updates to community boards.
