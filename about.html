About Fake News & Our Mission

Fake news is false or misleading information presented as news. Our mission is to equip communities with tools and skills to identify misinformation and preserve communal harmony.

Types of Fake News

  • Fabricated content: Completely false material created to deceive.
  • Misleading content: True facts presented out of context.
  • Imposter content: Fake pages or profiles pretending to be legitimate sources.
  • Manipulated media: Images/videos altered to change meaning.

Why It Spreads

  1. Emotionally charged headlines encourage immediate sharing.
  2. Algorithms amplify sensational content.
  3. Lack of digital literacy in many local communities.
  4. Echo chambers and confirmation bias reinforce false beliefs.
