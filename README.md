# Fake News Awareness – Spot & Verify

A web-based awareness project aimed at educating users and communities about fake news, misinformation, and responsible information sharing. The platform provides practical guidance, verification resources, and community-focused solutions to reduce the spread of false information.

## Project Overview

Fake news can cause panic, social division, and real-world harm. This project focuses on helping users understand how misinformation spreads and how to verify content before sharing. It is designed for students, communities, and awareness campaigns.

## Features

- Explanation of fake news and its real-world impact  
- Step-by-step guide to identify fake or misleading content  
- Fact-checking resources and downloadable awareness posters  
- Community awareness and local action strategies  
- Volunteer and contact section (demo form)  
- Multi-language support using Google Translate  

## Project Structure

- `index.html` – Home page  
- `about.html` – About fake news and project mission  
- `identify.html` – How to identify fake news  
- `factcheck.html` – Posters and fact-checking resources
- `tips.html` - Tips What to do 
- `community.html` – Community awareness and actions  
- `contact.html` – Volunteer and contact page  
- `css/style.css` – Styling and layout  
- `js/main.js` – Interactions and animations  

## Technologies Used
